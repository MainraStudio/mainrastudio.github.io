Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How long does game development take?

Development time depends on the project's complexity. A Student/Prototype package typically takes 1 weeks - 2 months, Professional takes 3-6 months, and Enterprise can take 8-12 months or more.

Can I modify an existing package?

Of course! We are very flexible in customizing packages to your specific needs. Please contact us for a free consultation, and we will help you create a suitable package.

Is the Student/Prototype package suitable for university assignments?

Yes, the Student/Prototype package is designed specifically for students and individuals who want to create a game prototype for learning, university assignments, or a portfolio. We also provide complete documentation for academic purposes.

What is included in post-launch support?

Post-launch support includes bug fixing, minor updates, and technical consultation. For Professional and Enterprise packages, we also provide performance analysis and improvement recommendations.
